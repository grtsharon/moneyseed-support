MoneySeed Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

Data Collection

MoneySeed does not require an account. MoneySeed does not collect, sell, or share personal financial data.

The values entered into the simulators are used on device to calculate projections and are not sent to a MoneySeed server.

Analytics and Tracking

MoneySeed does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Purchases

If paid features are added in a future version, purchases will be processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple may process purchase-related information according to Apple's own policies.

Children

MoneySeed is educational, but it is not specifically directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Financial Disclaimer

MoneySeed provides educational projections only. It is not financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Results are estimates and may differ from real-world outcomes.

Contact
